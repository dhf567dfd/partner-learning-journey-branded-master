  • Phishing 2018

    In just 15 minutes, you'll learn how to identify and protect against a phishing attack in your email box. Phishing—which involves the attempt to obtain usernames, passwords or other sensitive personal data via online solicitation from what appears to be a trustworthy source—is ranked consistently as the top method for hackers attempting to attack organizations and get confidential information. Each of us has a responsibility to protect the Firm and our clients from cyber threats. Technology isn't enough. We have spam filters and other electronic defenses, but those don't stop the most sophisticated - and riskiest - attacks. It's also up to you, and we want to arm you with the information you need to prevent phishing attacks on your devices. This video module is highly interactive and engaging, and it complements ongoing phishing training and exercises at the Firm. All Firm members are invited to use it. Colleagues who fail the Firm's regular phishing exercises may be required to take the course. Click here to download calendar reminder for this course.
